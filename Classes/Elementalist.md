#Class 
You have a knack for playing with the elements, using whatever suits the moment. You surpassed some of the greatest fighters when your second aspect manifested. Now, your control over the elements is rivaled by few.

## Level 1
Gain 1 of the following talents:
	[[Reconnaissance]]
	[[Magic Burst]]
	[[Professional]]

Unique Moves:
	Your standard weapon with the [[Attack]] action have -1 to the attack roll, but you have 5 extra special move points per level

Weapons proficiency:
	Gain proficiency in light weapons

Aspect:
	Gain 2 aspects. [[Lightning Aspect]] and [[Wind Aspect]] are recommended
## Level 2
Elemental Ball
	You can manifest your aspects into your hands, into the rough shape of a ball. You can throw this ball, or make an unarmed attack with it, dealing 1d4+INT damage of your element's type

## Level 3
Draw Power
	You can absorb the energy of an elemental attack and store it for later use. Once per long rest, you may absorb an attack or object of your aspects' type targeting you or forcing you to make a saving throw, and replenish one use of one of your special moves or heal 1d10 + your level. If it was an attack, you take half the damage rounded down. You cannot absorb an attack or object that consumed no use charges. You must expend 1 action point or 1 reaction point if in combat.
