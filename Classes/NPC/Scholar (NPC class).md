#NPC #Class
## Behavior

During downtime, studies by reading books, investigating objects or places, and talking to other scholars.

Has access to: Any educational, research, or knowledge institution that matches this character's studies

Possible studies: Combat, Literature, History, Logic, Medicine, Engineering


If this character does not have the Brave trait or Combat study
	Likes to stay out of combat, will only fight in self defense
	Dislikes violent characters
	In combat, will not actively seek enemies.
If this character does not have the Tinkerer trait or Engineering Study
	Likes to stay clean
	Dislikes dirty characters

Study Behaviors:
	Combat:
		Will use assess often, and target weak spots during combat
	Engineering:
		Will protect machines during combat

#### Attitude
+1 towards professors and other renowned figures
+2 towards scholars of the same study
+1 towards all other scholars
-3 towards people who ignore thinking
-3 towards people who attack educational institutions
+1 towards people who protect educational institutions
