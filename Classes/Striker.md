#Class 
You are the first to fight and the last to leave, taking down foe after foe with each powerful attack. You make every hit count, not relying on pure strength to break their defense, or pure finesse to avoid it.


## Level 1
Gain 1 of the following talents:
	[[Archer]]
	[[Polearm Fighter]]
	[[Swordsman]]
	[[Street Fighter]]

First Strike:
	In the first round of combat, you have double the action points you usually have. In addition, you suffer no penalty for attacking more than once.

Weapons proficiency:
	Gain proficiency in all weapons

Aspect:
	Gain 1 aspect. [[Fire Aspect]] recommended
## Level 2
Surgical Strike
	Your experience in combat lets you use your weapons with deadly precision. Once per long rest, if you miss an attack you can hit instead, turning it into a critical, or if you fail a STR based roll, you may turn it into a critical success.

Precision
	You have a number of d4s equal to twice your level called Precision Die. You may use Precision Die add to the damage die of an attack, add to the attack die, or add to any DEX based rolls. You replenish half of your Precision Die on long rests, and a quarter of your Precision Die on short rests rounding up.

## Level 3
Shatter
	When you hit with a critical, if the target is wearing armor or holding a shield, you may break their shield or armor and reduce their AC by 2 instead of dealing damage. Their shield or armor can be broken many times until you reduce its AC bonus to 0, by which it is shattered into pieces
