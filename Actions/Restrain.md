As an action, a target you can reach must succeed a DEX save or become restrained. Creatures two sizes larger than you have advantage on the saving throw

As an effect, the target becomes restrained by you

You cannot restrain a creature three sizes larger than you. You must have a free hand to restrain a creature smaller than you, and two free hands to restrain a creature the same size or larger.

On the target's turn, they may spend all their action points to make a STR saving throw. If they succeed, the Restrained condition ends on them.
