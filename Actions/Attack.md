Attack Stat: The stat you make the attack roll with
Damage Stat: The stat you gain a damage
Target: The creature being attacked

Damage Dice: The dice you roll for damage

Attack Bonus = Attack Stat + Proficiency
Damage Bonus = Damage Stat

When you take the attack action, you choose a target within range. If you attack with a projectile, it is considered a ranged attack, otherwise it is classified as a melee attack.

Your first roll is with a d20 with your attack bonus against the target's AC. If you hit, then you make a second roll to determine damage, which is equal to the result from rolling your damage dice + your damage bonus

If the Attack reduces the target to 0 health, you may choose to kill the target.

If you roll a 20 on your attack roll, the attack hits, and you roll your damage dice twice


## Additional Rules
If you don't know where your opponent is, you have to guess their location and attack with disadvantage

If you attack without a weapon, the attack stat is STR and the damage stat is STR. The damage die is 1d4
