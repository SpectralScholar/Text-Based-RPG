#NPC

Alignment: At least 1 of: Good, Chaotic
Skills: Climbing (+2 towards climbing, can carry more while climbing), Judo (with the redirection feat), Advanced Reading, Advanced Writing
Base Attitude Towards Player: +2
Agenda: Getting Smarter, Protecting all scholarly institutions.
Personality: Assertive, Brave, Smart
Likes: Intelligence
Dislikes: Brute Strength, Stealing

Flaws: 
	Nearsighted (-4 to perception, can't use reaction to defend against ranged attacks, treats attacks at more than 10 feet as blind attacks)
	Bad at Aiming (-4 to attack rolls for ranged attacks)
	Weak at unarmed strikes (Half damage to unarmed strikes, minimum 1. Does not apply to redirection)
Strengths:
	Increased accuracy against larger enemies (+2 to attack)
	Dexterous (+1 bonus to [[Dodge]] AC bonus, DEX checks, and DEX save DC)
	Patient (The [[Wait]] action gives 1 additional reaction point)

Behavior: Playful, unless there is a threat in the area of any kind that she is aware of.
Speech: Tends to talk in a casual tone. If talking to other scholars or important figures, will speak more formally
