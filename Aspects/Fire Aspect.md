#Aspect
Increase the level of all damage dice by 1, so a d4 becomes a d6, a d6 becomes a d8, and so on. For the d12, you gain an additional 1d4 

## Special Move Building

Effects:
	Fire (1 point)
	Burn effect (2 points)
	Explosion effect (5 points)
	Healing warmth aura (1 point per meter)
Costs:
	 You burn your 1d4 of your health (-3 points)
	 You burn a magic item (-4 points)
