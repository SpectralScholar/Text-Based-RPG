#Aspect 
Gain 1 extra reaction point per round of combat

## Special Move Building

Effects:
	Increase hearing range by 10 meters (2 points)
	Lightning Projectile (3 points)
	Discharge (3 points)
	Lighting Chain (2 point per max target)
Costs:
	Paralyzed for 1 turn (-2 points)
	Receive double damage while this move is active (-1 point per maximum turns active, up to -4)
