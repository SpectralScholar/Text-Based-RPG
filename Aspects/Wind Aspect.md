#Aspect 
Gain advantage on DEX checks and saving throws, +1 to AC when wearing light armor, +2 to attack rolls using DEX as the attack stat, and +1 to DEX saving throw DC.

## Special Move Building

Effects:
	Sharp gust of wind dealing slashing damage (1 points)
	Tornado (12 points per meter diameter)
	Wind protecting you against attacks (3 points)
Costs:
	Lose Balance and become prone (-2 points)
	Pushed in a random direction (-1 point per meter)

Combination with Lightning Aspect
	Sound
		Effects
			Intent
				Soundwave dealing Psychic damage (8 points + the scale factor of your move)
					Must use cone for effect range, damage dice diminishes by 1 level every 2 meters. Range is equal to 2 x total dice level. Affects all targets, including allies and enemies, within range.
