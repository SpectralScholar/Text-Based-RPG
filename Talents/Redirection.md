#Talent 
When attacked with a melee [[attack]], you can use a reaction point to replace the opponent's attack roll with a DEX save against their attacking stat. If you fail, you take an extra 1d6 damage. If you succeed, you take no damage and can choose one of the following effects for each free hand you have: [[Disarm]], [[Trip]] and deal 1 + DEX damage, Switch places with the attacker and deal 1 + STR damage, or [[Restrain]]. You can only take this reaction if you are not currently restraining, restrained, incapacitated, and have at least one free hand. 

Prerequisites: 14+ DEX
