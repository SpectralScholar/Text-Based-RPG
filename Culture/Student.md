#Culture
Growing up in school for most of your life, you have been accustomed to the safety and competition of a student body.

## Features
Talented
	Gain one talent of your choice that doesn't have any prerequisites 
Intelligent
	Increase INT by 2, and either PER or CHA by 1
Athletic
	Increase STR, DEX, or END by 1
Competitive
	Gain a +1 bonus towards and contest rolls
