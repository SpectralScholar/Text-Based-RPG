Weapons classes:
	Light weapons
	Medium weapons
	Heavy weapons
Weapon types:
	Ranged weapons:
		Bows (Requires PER on the attack roll and STR on the damage roll)
		Slings (Requires DEX on the attack and damage rolls)
		Trigger shot weapons (Requires PER on the attack roll and DEX on the damage roll)
	Melee weapons:
		Polearms (STR on attack and damage rolls)
		Swords (DEX on attack roll, STR on damage roll)
			If heavy, uses STR on the attack roll
		Hammers (STR on attack and damage rolls)
		Axes (STR on attack and damage rolls)
		Knives (DEX on the attack and damage rolls)
		Bracers, Knuckles, and other unarmed enhancing weapons (Use Unarmed Rules)
	Aspect weapons:
		Any of the above, can use either those attack rolls and damage rolls, or substitute out attack, damage, or both rolls for INT.

for ranged attacks, -3 to the attack for each increment of the range

Weapon Features
	Two hands
		If you use two hands, the damage increases by one step
	Sweep
		This weapon attacks one additional creature adjacent to you and diagonal to the original target
	Ranged
		A projectile is shot out of the weapon. If attempting to attack outside of the weapon's range, take a -3 penalty. If attacking outside twice the weapon's range, take a -6 penalty. Cannot shoot further than thrice the weapon's range
	Reach
		The weapon's melee range is increased to the distance listed
